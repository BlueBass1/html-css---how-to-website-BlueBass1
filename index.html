Welcome to my how-to guide on how to get started gaming. Gaming is a very open ended hobby that allows you to explore many different experiences.

As someone who's looking to pick up the hobby, There are some questions that you should ask yourself.

  1. What Platform would you like to play on?

    2. Deciding what you want to play games on is important. When making this decision, you want to consider whether what you want out of your platform. Do you want something to play at home. Maybe with your family? If so, then buying a Nintendo console would be great choice. If you want something more customimizable and personal, then getting a PC for gaming can be a better choice. There are a few more options available out there than the two mentitoned here, but staying in the scope of consoles and PC will be all you need to get started.

  2. What genre of games intrest you?

    3. Now the other important question is what type game would you like to play. Arguably the best aspect of gaming is that there is something for anyone who wants play. As I've mentitoned before, this is a very open ended hobby. Games range from relaxing exploration and story driven adventures, to non-stop action games and fast-paced racing games.
